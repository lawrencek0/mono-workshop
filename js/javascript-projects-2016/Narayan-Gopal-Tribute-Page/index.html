Emperor of Voice - Narayan Gopal

Here's a time line of Narayan Gopal's life:

  • 1939 - Born in Kathmandu, Nepal
  • 1960 - Completed Secondary School
  • 1965 - Visits Darjeeling, India
  • 1966 - Recieves Best Singer (Radio Nepal)
  • 1967 - Receives Best Singer (Radio Nepal) award
  • 1969 - Receives Narayan Gopal Singing Award
  • 1971 - Marries Pemala Lama
  • 1976 - Receives Gorkha Dakshin Bahu, Fourth
  • 1982 - Receives Ratna Record Award
  • 1983 - Receives Indra Rajya Laxmi Award
  • 1987 - Receives Chhinalata Award
  • 1988 - Receives Jagadamba Shree
  • 1990 - Receives Urbashi Rang Award
  • 1990 - Dies at the age of 51
  • 1991- Receives Trishakti Patta, Third (posthumously)
